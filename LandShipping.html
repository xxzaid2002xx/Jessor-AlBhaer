Land Shipping

Jessor Al-Bahar Shipping is one of the leading land shipping companies in Iraq and the Arabian Gulf, having provided outstanding land shipping services for many years. The company offers a large and diverse fleet of trucks covering the GCC countries and the Middle East, with the necessary expertise to provide cargo insurance and specialized services for large companies and projects.

Land transport and shipping are characterized by flexibility in meeting customer demands and logistical needs, as well as easy access to shipment pick-up and delivery points. Jessor Al-Bahar offers a variety of light, heavy, and refrigerated trucks depending on the nature of the shipments, along with car carriers that safely load and transport cars across borders.

Types of Light and Heavy Trucks by Load and Length:

  • 3 tons, 5 tons, 10 tons, 25 tons, 12.5 meters, 13.6 meters, 15 meters, 18 meters
  • All of these are available in various configurations such as fully enclosed, refrigerated, open-topped, and open-sided trucks, as well as heavy-duty trucks (lowbeds) with capacities up to 85 tons and car carriers with a capacity of up to 8 cars.

Jessor Al-Bahar offers reliable and cost-effective land shipping services in Iraq with the best multi-purpose vehicles. There's no need to worry about your shipments as our specialized team will handle the delivery to your destination anywhere in the Gulf, the Middle East, and North Africa.
