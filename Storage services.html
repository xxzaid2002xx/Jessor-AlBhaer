Storage Services

Jessor Al Behar provides integrated storage services that meet the needs of clients in Iraq and the GCC countries. Our services are distinguished by offering a secure and monitored environment for storing various goods, whether commercial, industrial, or sensitive materials requiring specific temperature conditions.

We offer flexible storage solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of the market, including storing large or small goods, as well as perishable materials and sensitive products requiring special storage conditions. Our services rely on modern technologies to ensure the safety and protection of goods throughout the storage period.

Types of Storage Services Offered by Jessor Al Behar:

  • Storage of perishable food products in refrigerated warehouses.
  • Storage of commercial goods and industrial products.
  • Safe storage of heavy equipment and industrial machinery.
  • Storage of hazardous materials in compliance with international standards.
  • Storage of household furniture and furnishings in a protected environment.
  • Storage of goods in secure, closed warehouses that protect them from external factors.
  • Short-term and long-term storage services according to customer needs.

Jessor Al Behar aims to provide the highest levels of service by offering warehouses equipped with the latest technologies to ensure proper storage of goods. We place great emphasis on providing a safe storage environment, with the ability to monitor shipments closely to ensure they reach their final destination in the best condition.

  • Warehouses equipped with modern facilities to ensure safe and flexible storage.
