Domestic Goods Transport

Jassoor Al-Bahar is a leading company in the field of domestic goods transport in Iraq and the Gulf countries. The company provides highly efficient internal transportation services, with a diverse fleet of trucks offering flexible and safe solutions for transporting goods within Iraq and the region.

The domestic transport services provided by Jassoor Al-Bahar excel in delivering innovative logistical solutions that contribute to speeding up the movement of goods and reducing transportation costs. The company offers fast and safe transportation services through a diverse fleet of trucks suitable for all types of goods based on size and weight.

Types of Trucks Used in Domestic Transport:

  • Light and medium trucks for city transportation.
  • Heavy trucks for transporting large and bulky goods.
  • Refrigerated trucks for transporting food and products that require low temperatures.
  • Open and closed trucks to ensure goods are protected from external elements during transport.
  • Special trucks for transporting heavy equipment and industrial machinery.

Jassoor Al-Bahar provides ideal solutions for transporting goods internally across Iraq and the Gulf countries by offering a variety of trucks tailored to different shipping needs, including the transport of food, industrial, and commercial products. Domestic transport services are an essential part of Jassoor Al-Bahar's strategy to offer comprehensive logistical solutions that meet the needs of both the local and international markets.
