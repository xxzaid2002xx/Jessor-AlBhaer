What is the Harmonized System HSCOD?

It is an international multipurpose nomenclature for goods, developed by the World Customs Organization. It is also called the Harmonized System and is used to determine the applicable customs duty rate for any commodity. It consists of a table including items, chapters, and their numerical codes and is used as a basis for tariffs in more than 176 countries or customs unions.

Advantages of Applying the Harmonized System:

  • The Harmonized System has helped governments establish an accurate mechanism for determining duties and taxes on goods.
  • The Harmonized System has facilitated the process of providing and preparing accurate information for investors and researchers.
  • All goods in the Harmonized System fall under clear classifications, which facilitates the import and export process.
